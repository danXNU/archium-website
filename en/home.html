FAQ

Answers to common questions before you start.

Sharing clear answers removes friction at the final decision point. We aim to address the most important topics, especially around security, and onboarding.

Do you collect any personal data?

As our goal is to keep things simple. That's why we don't collect any data. And we won't be doing in future.

How do you organize documents?

Archium uses Apple Intelligence to automatically categorize your documents and organize them into smart folders, keeping everything tidy and easy to find. All processing happens locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and full control over your data.

Will iCloud support be added?

Yes, iCloud integration is on our roadmap. We're working to make it simple for users to sync documents across Apple devices. Stay tuned for updates!
